BACKGROUND

Family Safety is a security app that keeps families safe with advanced location sharing, alerts and messaging. Busy families need a product that provides location tracking, speed monitoring and safety measures to ensure their independent children remain safe. With an acute understanding of product features, I leveraged user research to develop personas, user journeys and interaction design prototypes for the speed monitoring feature.
